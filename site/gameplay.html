Gameplay

Cricket being a game rich with history has many greats and talented people. Known as the greatest batsmen of all time and the highest run scorer in the world, Sachin Tendulkar. Sachin was a child prodigy known for his exceptional skills in batting and when he first started for the Indian national team, he was slowly living upto his reputation. He ended up becoming the highest century scorer with 100 centuries and highest run scorer with 34,357 runs. Another great of the game is Virat Kohli who is this generations' talent. Known as the king of the game, he has accumalated 26,733 runs and 80 centuries.
